If entertainment was a buffet, then Cory "Zooman" Miller  would offer just about every dish one could ever desire. Aside from being a headlining comedian and President Obama impressionist, Miller can sing, rap, dance, act and produce. With more talent than he knows what to do with, Cory has managed to incorporate all of his abilities into one entertaining act, regardless of how it's classified. Jersey-born, he attended The Art Institute of Atlanta where his dance and battle rap skills flourished. Writing for Lil’ Zane and choreographing for multi-platinum producer Dallas Austin.
Stand up comedy was the next dish. Two years in Cory landed multiple appearances on the HBO Original Series, “Russell Simmons’ Def Comedy Jam”, BET Network’s “Comic View” and resident comedian on "The Mike Roberts Morning Show" on Atlanta’s V-103. Catching the eye of Hollywood, he rose even higher. Regular host of NBC’s late night talk show “Later”, The Improv Comedy Club Comedy Store.

Further credits include Starz Network “First Amendment”(comic) & “Ryan Cameron’s Uncensored”(writer/sketch), Comedy Central’s “Premium Blend”(Comic) & “Jamie Foxx’s Laffapalooza”(Comic) HBO’s P. Diddy’s “Bad Boys of Comedy” Tours include Katt Williams Conspiracy Theory & Great America, Rickey Smiley & Friends, HBO Def Comedy Jam, Sprite Soul Comedy Tour & various US military bases in Germany, England and Japan.

Complex Magazine named Cory as the #2 best ever, President Obama impressionist. Zooman Recently wrapped shooting Hidden Empire Film Group’s “Meet The Blacks 2” alongside Mike Epps, Katt Williams, Danny Trejo and Rick Ross.
